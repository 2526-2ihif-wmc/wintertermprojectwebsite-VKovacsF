About the Mixtape

King of the Mischievous South Vol. 2 (shortened to KOTMS II) is the debut commercial mixtape by American rapper Denzel Curry, released on July 19, 2024, via PH Recordings and Loma Vista.

It serves as the sequel to his third mixtape King of the Mischievous South Vol. 1 Underground Tape 1996 (2012), and also serves as the follow-up to Curry's fifth studio album, Melt My Eyez See Your Future (2022).

Production

The mixtape features guest appearances from Kingpin Skinny Pimp, Key Nyata, Maxo Kream, TiaCorine, ASAP Ferg, That Mexican OT, 2 Chainz, Mike Dimes, Kenny Mason, Project Pat, Ty Dolla Sign, Juicy J, Armani White, Ski Mask the Slump God, ASAP Rocky, and PlayThatBoiZay.

The Repackage (Deluxe edition)

On October 30, Curry announced a deluxe edition of the mixtape, which he described as an album, with five additional tracks, it was released on November 15 as King of the Mischievous South; it includes additional guest appearances from Duke Deuce, Slim Guerilla, 454, Sauce Walka, Bktherula, and Lazer Dim 700.

Critical Reception

According to the review aggregator Metacritic, King of the Mischievous South Vol. 2 received "generally favorable reviews" based on a weighted average score of 74 out of 100 from 8 critic scores.

"Having long since proven his skill as a lyricist, Curry is more concerned with having a good time here. And that also makes for a hell of a good listen."

-Steve Erickson's review from Slant-
