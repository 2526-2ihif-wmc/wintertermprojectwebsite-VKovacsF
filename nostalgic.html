NOSTALGIC 64

Released September 3, 2013

About the Album

Nostalgic 64 is the debut studio album by American rapper Denzel Curry. It was released on September 3, 2013.

Production

Producers who contributed to this album include Rem, Ronny J, MarkMC9, Poshstronaut, Klvn Tyler, Nuri, Steven A. Clark, J Green, Freebase, Lofty305 and Sydneyintheory.

Critical Reception

Nostalgic 64 received widespread critical acclaim upon its release. Mike Madden from Pitchfork Magazine listed it as one of the top 10 albums of 2013 with Threatz listed as the top individual track as well.

Lee Castro of Miami New Times called it "one of the best rap projects to come out of Miami in 2013".
